Secondary evidence (downstream effects or noise):
- Evidence 3: Node-level resource usage increases (memory and CPU) on several nodes – these are likely secondary effects or correlated noise, as they are less severe and could be consequences of the primary storage issue or unrelated background processes.

Step 3: Convergence to conclusion
"The combination of the storage metric anomaly (region_pending spike) and the specific trace delays in services dependent on that storage indicates that the root cause is a performance issue in the storage component, where accumulated pending operations lead to slow data access and cascading delays in upstream services."

[Conclusion]
