Secondary evidence (downstream effects or noise):

[Reasoning]

Step 2: Verification or elimination
"If this were caused by an application-level bug in a specific service, we would expect to see error logs or trace patterns unique to that service's logic; however, actual observation shows broad performance degradation across multiple services located on the same node. Step 3: Convergence to conclusion
"The combination of the node-level storage metric exhaustion and the correlated timing delays in traces for services hosted on that node indicates a root cause where a shared infrastructure resource became saturated, creating a bottleneck for multiple dependent applications."

[Conclusion]
