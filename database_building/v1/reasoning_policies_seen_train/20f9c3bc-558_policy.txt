Step 3: Convergence to conclusion
"The combination of internal runtime errors in the currency service logs and the trace evidence of cascading call failures points to the currency service's inability to handle requests due to a computational logic flaw. This failure mode explains the observed latency spikes and cancellation errors across dependent services."
