Step 3: Convergence to conclusion
"The combination of direct trace failures indicating paymentservice inaccessibility and corroborating logs from dependent services showing the same error pattern indicates that the root cause is a service accessibility problem at the paymentservice."

[Conclusion]
"The root cause is a configuration or operational issue at the paymentservice that prevents successful network connections, leading to cascading failures in dependent services."
